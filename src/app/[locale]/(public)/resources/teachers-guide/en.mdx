## Native Land Digital Teacher’s Guide 2025

<div class="mt-4 nld-text-lg nld-text-teal-500">Welcome to the Native Land Digital Teacher’s Guide!</div>

This resource is designed to support educators in using Native-Land.ca as a starting point for learning about Indigenous territories, languages, treaties, and, most importantly, the relationships Indigenous peoples have with Land & Waters.

<div class="border-l-4 nld-border-brown-500 nld-bg-brown-50 p-4 mt-8">
  <div class="uppercase nld-text-brown-500 nld-font-jost nld-font-h5">Our Goal</div>

Our goal is to move beyond static land acknowledgments and towards active engagement with Indigenous histories, governance systems, and ways of knowing. This guide will help you facilitate meaningful conversations, encourage critical thinking, and provide hands-on activities that connect students to the Land & Waters around them.
</div>
